# AllFix Maintenance Services

## Product Overview

AllFix is a service marketplace platform for home and office maintenance services in Islamabad. It enables customers to browse, select, and order various maintenance and cleaning services across multiple categories.

## Core Features

- **Service Catalog**: Organized hierarchical catalog of services (e.g., AC services, appliances, carpentry, electrical, cleaning, etc.)
- **Service Categories**: Main categories with subcategories (e.g., "Home Services" contains appliances, carpenter, electrician, geyser, handyman, painter, plumber, etc.)
- **Shopping Cart**: Add services to cart and manage orders
- **Service Details**: Browse individual services with descriptions and pricing
- **Contact & Inquiries**: Contact page for customer inquiries
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first design supporting all device sizes

## Target Users

- Homeowners and office managers in Islamabad seeking maintenance services
- Service seekers looking for professional cleaning and maintenance solutions

## Key Service Categories

- AC Services (installation, repair, maintenance)
- Appliances (washing machine, oven, cook range, hood)
- Carpentry (doors, drawers, furniture, locks)
- Electrical (wiring, fans, lights, switches, UPS, pumps)
- Geyser Services (electric, gas, instant)
- Handyman Services (mounting, hanging, shelving)
- Painting (indoor, outdoor, furniture polish)
- Plumbing (leaks, fixtures, installations)
- Cleaning (sofa, carpet, mattress, deep cleaning)
- Water Tanks (underground, rooftop, plastic, cement)
- Solar Services
- CCTV Installation

## Business Model

Service-based marketplace where customers browse and order services. Services are organized hierarchically for easy discovery.
